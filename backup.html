Community-Based Monitoring Network

A. Community-Based Monitoring Network

Local volunteers, youth groups, farmers, and community leaders are trained to observe and record early-warning indicators:

  • Crop stress, delayed rains, and pest or disease outbreaks
  • Livestock mortality or reduced productivity
  • Declining household food stocks
  • Price spikes for staple foods
  • Water scarcity and WASH vulnerabilities
  • Nutritional changes among infants and young children
